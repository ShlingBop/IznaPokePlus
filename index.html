About Izna Poke Plus

The Poké Plus family is here to bring you a beloved Hawaian classic with Japanese + Korean twist! Come enjoy delicious Poké bowls PLUS our own Korean style poke bowls! We only serve with the freshest ingredients and sashimi grade fish, shipped daily to our store. Great balance of produce and proteins all in one bowl, with amazing flavour!
